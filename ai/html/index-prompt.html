AI Safety Tips

  • Verify Outputs Always fact-check AI-generated content before publishing or acting on it — AI can confidently state incorrect information.
  • Protect Personal Data Never enter sensitive info (SSNs, passwords, private health records) into public AI tools.
  • Clarify Your Intent Be explicit about your goals. Ambiguous prompts lead to unpredictable or off-target responses.
  • Know the Limits AI models have knowledge cutoffs and can't browse the web unless explicitly given that capability.
  • Use Responsibly Avoid using AI to generate misleading content, impersonate others, or produce harmful material.
  • Review Before Sending Always read AI-drafted messages carefully — tone, accuracy, and final content are your responsibility.
